Trip Summery

If you have not been to South America, you must go! Peru has a great history, beautiful (and diverse) geography and friendly people. Classic tourist attractions here include the magnificent Machu Pichu and the metropolis of Lima.

Program

  • Day 1: Tour of metro Lima, and the museums
  • Day 2: Tour of the historical district and some great museums
  • Day 3: A ride out to the Cuzco region, then to Machu Pichu and the surrounding mountains, and a place to stay in Cuzco by night
  • Day 4: A zip lining adventure in the mountains, and a traditional guinea pig roast
